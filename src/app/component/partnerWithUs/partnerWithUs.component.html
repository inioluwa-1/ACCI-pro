Together, we can create lasting change and build a better future for all.

At Action for Collective Change (ACCI), we believe that sustainable development requires strong partnerships. We work with organizations, businesses, and institutions that share our vision of creating a more equitable and prosperous Africa.

By partnering with ACCI, you're not just supporting a cause. You're joining a network of changemakers committed to empowering youth, promoting gender equality, driving sustainable development, and shaping policy for a more just and equitable society.

Why Partner with ACCI?

  • • Amplify Your Impact: Leverage our established network and expertise to maximize your organization's social impact.
  • • Access New Markets: Connect with communities and stakeholders across Africa through our extensive network.
  • • Shared Resources: Combine strengths and resources to tackle complex development challenges more effectively.
  • • Sustainable Solutions: Co-create innovative solutions that address pressing social and environmental issues.

Who Can Partner With Us?

We welcome partnerships with organizations across all sectors - from corporations and foundations to NGOs and academic institutions. If you share our commitment to sustainable development and social impact, we'd love to explore how we can work together.

Partnership Opportunities Include:

  • Corporate Social Responsibility Initiatives
  • Research & Development Collaborations
  • Capacity Building Programs
  • Community Development Projects
  • Advocacy & Policy Development
  • Resource Mobilization & Fundraising
