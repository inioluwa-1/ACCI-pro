Our Story

Action For Collective Change Initiative (ACCI)

Action for Collective Change Initiative (ACCI) is a U.S.-based 501(c)(3) grassroots non-profit organization committed to creating positive sustainable changes in communities worldwide. ACCI addresses social, economic, and environmental challenges through community-driven programs, empowering individuals, communities, and societies to thrive through local action and live better narratives.

MISSION STATEMENT

EMPOWER COMMUNITIES, INDIVIDUALS, AND SOCIETIES TO BECOME AGENTS OF CHANGE BY CREATING COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTIONS THAT ADDRESS INJUSTICE, AND ENVIRONMENTAL, SOCIAL, AND ECONOMIC CHALLENGES AND PROMOTE A SUSTAINABLE WORLD WHERE ALL CAN THRIVE.

VISION

ACCOUNTABILITY, EQUITY, ADAPTABILITY, COLLABORATION, RESPONSIBILITY AND TRANSPARENCY.

OUR VALUES

OUR VALUES GUIDE EVERYTHING WE DO, ENSURING WE REMAIN ADAPTABLE TO THE EVOLVING NEEDS OF OUR COMMUNITIES AND WORLD. WE UPHOLD ACCOUNTABILITY THROUGH TRANSPARENCY AND RESPONSIBILITY IN ALL OUR ACTIONS. COMMITTED TO EQUITY, WE STRIVE TO CREATE AN INCLUSIVE AND FAIR ENVIRONMENT FOR EVERYONE. THROUGH COLLABORATION, WE WORK HAND IN HAND WITH LOCAL COMMUNITIES, NONPROFITS, AND STAKEHOLDERS TO DRIVE MEANINGFUL CHANGE AND ACHIEVE SHARED GOALS.

Our Objectives

At Action for Collective Change Initiative (ACCI), we are committed to empowering communities and driving sustainable change through:

  • Education Access – Providing scholarships, online learning, and partnerships to promote lifelong learning.
  • Youth Sports Development – Supporting school and community sports to foster discipline, teamwork, and excellence.
  • Entrepreneurship & Economic Growth – Offering business training, mentorship, and micro-grants to help entrepreneurs thrive.
  • Climate Action – Promoting environmental awareness and youth-led solutions for sustainability.
  • Digital Literacy – Equipping young people with essential tech skills to succeed in today’s digital world.
  • Social Justice – Advocating for equity and amplifying marginalized voices.
  • Global Alignment – Ensuring all our work contributes meaningfully to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
