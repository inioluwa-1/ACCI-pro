Action for Collective Change (ACCI)

At Action for Collective Change (ACCI), we believe that real change begins with people—passionate, committed, and community-driven individuals who are willing to give their time, skills, and energy to create a better future.

By volunteering with us, you're not just signing up for an activity. You're becoming part of a movement that empowers youth, promotes gender equality, drives sustainable development, and shapes policy for a more just, equitable, and prosperous Africa.

Why Volunteer with ACCI?

  • • Make Real Impact: Contribute directly to projects that uplift communities and transform lives across the world
  • • Build New Skills: Gain hands-on experience in areas such as education, advocacy, sustainability, and project implementation
  • • Grow Your Network: Connect with like-minded changemakers, development professionals, and partner organizations
  • • Be a Voice for Change: Help shape conversations and drive initiatives around decent work, digital inclusion, gender equality, and more

Who Can Volunteer?

Whether you're a student, professional, retiree, or simply someone with a heart for service, there's a place for you at ACCI. We welcome volunteers with diverse backgrounds and experiences who are committed to advancing the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Africa.

Volunteer Opportunities Include:

  • Community Outreach & Advocacy
  • Project Coordination & Support
  • Communications & Content Creation
  • Research & Policy Development
  • Fundraising & Event Planning
  • Technical Skills Training

...and more!
